The brief

Beyond insurance — into health

Medibank is one of Australia’s largest private health insurers, with over four million members. Its core business operates in a regulated, competitive market with constrained margins. The strategic imperative was to diversify revenue by moving into adjacent health and wellbeing services — establishing the capability and product to serve employers and their employees in a way no insurer had done before.

The Corporate Health and Wellbeing market represented a significant opportunity — Australian employers spending billions annually on employee health programs, with fragmented delivery and no clear digital-native leader. The question was whether Medibank could establish the organisational capability to move fast enough to lead that space.

The approach

Establishing the capability from the ground up

I was the first hire into this venture — brought in to establish everything from nothing. The mandate: define the product vision and operating model, validate the opportunity, build the team, and take the venture from exploration through to commercial launch inside a highly regulated healthcare environment.

The product was built on League — the leading healthcare consumer experience platform reaching over 20 million people globally. Medibank selected League as its infrastructure partner for corporate digital health and wellbeing. The partnership, initially focused on this corporate venture, was announced publicly in October 2024.

In parallel, I defined Medibank’s enterprise AI product strategy — a three-year roadmap shaping how AI could be embedded across member-facing and clinical experiences for four million members, including AI Health Assessment, Find a Provider, Symptom Checker and Next Best Action initiatives.

The product

Work Better by Medibank

The result of this venture is Work Better by Medibank — a corporate health and wellbeing app available to employees whose workplace has partnered with the platform, regardless of whether they hold private health insurance.

The app delivers: bite-sized health content across exercise, women’s health, mental health, sleep and nutrition; workplace challenges and team competitions; health metric tracking synced with existing health apps; a clinician-backed health assessment; and an AI facial scanning feature delivering real-time stress, heart rate, and HRV insights in under a minute.
