The brief

Beyond insurance — into health

Medibank is one of Australia’s largest private health insurers, with over four million members. Its core business — health insurance — operates in a regulated, competitive market with constrained margins. The strategic imperative was to diversify revenue by moving into adjacent health and wellbeing services that members, and employers, would genuinely value and pay for.

The Corporate Health and Wellbeing market represented a significant opportunity — Australian employers spending billions annually on employee health programs, with a fragmented landscape, inconsistent delivery, and no clear digital-native leader. The question was whether Medibank could move fast enough to own that space.

The approach

First hire. Blank page.

I was the first hire into this venture — brought in to build from nothing. The mandate: take Corporate Health from exploration through to commercial launch, validating the opportunity, defining the product, building the team, and integrating the venture into Medibank’s strategic, clinical, and commercial frameworks. All simultaneously, in a highly regulated healthcare environment.

The product was built on League — the leading healthcare consumer experience platform, which Medibank selected as its infrastructure partner for corporate digital health and wellbeing. League’s platform powers health experiences for over 20 million people globally. Medibank’s partnership with League, focused initially on the corporate product, was announced in October 2024 — the same platform underpinning Work Better by Medibank.

In parallel, I defined Medibank’s enterprise AI product strategy — a three-year roadmap shaping how AI could be embedded across member-facing and clinical experiences for four million members, including AI Health Assessment, Find a Provider, Symptom Checker and Next Best Action initiatives.

The product

Work Better by Medibank

Work Better by Medibank is the employee health and wellbeing app that came out of this venture. It’s available to employees whose workplace has partnered with Work Better — regardless of whether they hold Medibank private health insurance.

Inside: bite-sized health content across exercise, mental health, sleep and nutrition; workplace challenges; health metric tracking synced with existing health apps; a clinician-backed health assessment; and AI facial scanning for real-time health insights. It’s a complete employee wellness platform, built from the ground up inside a regulated healthcare environment, live in market.
